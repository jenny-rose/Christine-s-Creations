Christine's Creations

Repairs and Alterations

Repairs and alterations can be performed on most any garments, including heavier materials such as leather. Don't give up on your favorite jeans or that new dress! I can alter sleeve lengths; replace buttons, zippers, and hooks; replace liners, hem pants, and resize bridal and prom dresses.

Custom Garments

Can't find quite the right fit or drape? I can draft and create custom garments, including jackets, vest, slacks, and gowns. I have several clients that I've been clothing with custom suits for years, and I've also worked on a line of handbags for a local leather store here in Ohio.

Bikers Welcome!

Got patches? I will sew on patches according to club or individual specifications. Bring in your patches and vest and I'll work with you to make sure patches are located exactly where you want them. I can also remove the liner prior to sewing for an additional fee (in case you don't want stitching to show on the liner).

About Christine

I've been a working seamstress for 30+ years, trained formally in Germany and now serving the northeastern Ohio area with needle and thread in hand! If you're in Canton, Massillon, or the surrounding area, contact me to give your wardrobe a little handmade, homegrown TLC.
